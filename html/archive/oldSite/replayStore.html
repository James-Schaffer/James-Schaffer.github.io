About This Game

You make your way through the levels with a double jump (Space) and dash (LShift) at your disposal. Avoid the spikes or falling off the platform and use the gravity inverters and your momentum to your advantage to get through.

This was made for Brackeys Game Jam 2023.1. It is a themed, week long, game making competiotion. It was the first time I had done a game jam and it was something I enjoyed and hope to do again. There were 738 entrites and I placed 361st.
