Hello! Welcome to my website


I'm James Schaffer, a student at the University of Leicester studying Computer Science and an aspiring game developer. I grew up playing games and now it's my mission to make them. I have been self teaching game development for around 4 years, learning to use the Unity and Godot game engines as well as supporting tools and software. In this time I have participated in 2 game jams and been nominated for the 2023 BAFTA young game maker award.
